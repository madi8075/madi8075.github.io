Hi, I’m Madison Manuel.

In my sophomore year of high school, a friend asked me to take a Java programming course with her. I had no experience nor any plan to major in Computer Science, but after writing my first “hello, world!” program I was hooked. With a newly discovered aptitude for Java, my teacher soon started sending me to help fellow classmates. This semester was the spark of my passion in Computer Science.

Through dual enrollment, I began college at EFSC while taking that class. With how quickly I had become passionate about the subject, my choice of major was clear. Later at UCF, I spent time exploring the variety of fields within Computer Science. Last year, I found another passion within Cybersecurity and this quickly became my minor.

While looking for a job related to technology, I found Asurion Tech Repair & Solutions. I currently work as a device repair technician and sales expert. Over the last two years the position has strengthened my teamwork, patience, software skills, and analytical mindset. I am eager for the opportunity to apply my current knowledge in a career and learn what only more experience can teach.
